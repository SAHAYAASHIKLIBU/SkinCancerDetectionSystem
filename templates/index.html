About the Application

This Flask-based web application is designed for skin cancer detection, focusing on identifying three skin conditions: Seborrheic Keratosis (SK), Actinic Keratosis (AK), and Basal Cell Carcinoma (BCC). The application utilizes a machine learning model trained exclusively on dermoscopy images to perform image classification. Users can upload dermoscopy images, and the system will process and classify the image into one of the three conditions.
